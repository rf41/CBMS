How It Works

  1. Upload Data: Go to the Dashboard and upload a JSON file containing sensor readings
  2. Analysis: Our system analyzes 5 key behavioral indicators from the sensor data
  3. Risk Assessment: Get a risk score and status: Low Risk, Needs Observation, or Professional Consultation
  4. Recommendations: Receive tailored guidance based on the assessment results
